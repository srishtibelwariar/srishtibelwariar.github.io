With Passion comes Purpose

Hi there! My name is Srishti and I'm a freshman at Cornell University (College of Engineering). When it comes to all things tech, from web design and development to tinkering with programming languages and software, I'm always looking for a new project to get behind of and work on. I strive for learning through the process rather than quickly reaching the end result, which allows me to explore many areas of tech with an open mind. Aside from technology, I am also passionate about my work in fine arts. I have been playing the Sitar, an Indian classical instrument for over 5 years and have received certifications that count towards a university degree. I live by the idea that passion and dedication provides one purpose and direction. My passion and dedication to learning and exploring numerous areas in technology and the arts builds my purpose. Thank you for visiting my website and please do check out some of my works and projects.
