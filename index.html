About Me

Hi there! I'm Srishti and I'm a sophomore at Cornell University (College of Engineering). When it comes to all things tech, from developing and designing websites to tinkering with programming languages and software to exploring new applications in VUI, I'm always looking for a new project to get behind and work on. I strive for learning through the process rather than quickly reaching the end result, which allows me to explore many areas of tech with an open mind. I live by the idea that passion provides purpose and direction and I strive to apply this notion to my work and experiences. Thank you for visiting my website and please do check out some of my works and projects.
